Privacy Policy for Tbani

Effective Date: 12 August 2025
Last Updated: 12 August 2025

Important: This policy explains how we handle your data in Tbani, a pet adoption and lost/found pets platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

a. Authentication Information

  • Account Information: Email address, phone number, display name, profile picture
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials, authentication tokens (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Optional Profile Data: Date of birth, gender (if provided during registration)

b. Pet-Related Content Data

  • Post Information: Pet descriptions, photos, animal type, category (lost/found/adoption)
  • User Content: Messages, comments, favorites, adoption status updates
  • Interaction Data: Posts you view, favorite, or interact with

c. Location Data

  • Approximate Location: General area for showing nearby pets and posts
  • Post Location: Specific location where a pet was found or lost (only with your explicit permission)
  • Note: We never track your continuous location. Location access is optional and used only when posting or browsing nearby pets.

d. Technical and Usage Data

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, app version
  • Usage Analytics: Features used, time spent, actions taken (to improve app experience)
  • Behavioral Data: What content you view, click on, or engage with to understand user preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like pet posting, searching, and adoption matching
  • Personalization: To show relevant pets and content based on your location and preferences
  • Analytics & Improvement: To analyze user behavior and improve app features and user experience
  • Communication: To send important updates, adoption matches, and support responses
  • Safety & Security: To prevent fraud, abuse, and ensure community guidelines are followed

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the services you request
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our app and prevent fraud
  • Consent: For optional features like location services and analytics

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. We only share data in these limited circumstances:

a. Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, data storage, and analytics
  • Cloud Services: For hosting and infrastructure

b. Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To prevent fraud or abuse of our services

c. Public Information

  • Pet posts you create may be visible to other app users
  • Your display name and profile picture may be shown with your posts

5. Third-Party Services

Tbani uses these trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication – Secure user authentication Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Firestore – Cloud database for storing app data
  • Firebase Analytics – Anonymous usage analytics Data Processing Terms
  • Google Cloud Platform – Infrastructure and hosting services

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Data is kept while your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be deleted after 24 months of inactivity
  • Post Data: Pet posts are retained to maintain adoption records
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated analytics data may be kept indefinitely in anonymized form

7. Your Rights and Choices

a. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time directly within the app:

  • Go to Profile → Account → Delete Account
  • This will permanently remove your personal information
  • Your public posts may be retained for community safety records

b. Data Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

c. Communication Preferences

  • Control push notifications through your device settings
  • Opt-out of marketing communications (if any are sent)

8. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encryption: Data in transit using TLS/SSL encryption
  • Authentication: Secure authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Access Controls: Role-based access to sensitive data
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and monitoring
Security Notice: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We continuously work to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Service provider compliance with international data protection standards

10. Children's Privacy

Tbani is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will:

  • Notify you of significant changes through the app
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Provide a summary of changes for major updates
